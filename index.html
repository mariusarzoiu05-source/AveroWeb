Does Avero store my links?

Privacy-first by design. You can run scans without building a personal browsing history.

What does the risk score mean?

It’s a simple indicator from low to high risk, based on signals like domain patterns, suspicious keywords, and urgency tactics.

Is this a browser extension or an app?

It can be both. Start as a mobile-first experience and expand to extensions and team dashboards later.
